Welcome to the Super 322 Drive-In!

There aren't many drive-in theaters left in America, but we're proud to be one of them! Each show is a double feature beginning after sunset. Featured films are first-run, family-friendly, and very affordable. A full concession stand serves visitors with a variety of traditional drive-in fare. No matter what the weather, the show goes on!

Admission Price

Adult - $8 per person
Children (11 and under) - $4 per person
At this time, we only accept cash
